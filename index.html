Gino Bona

I was born in Buffalo.

I lived in Virginia, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Maine.

I now live in Raleigh with my wife, two children and our coprophagic Labrador Retriever.

Hustle is underrated.

There is nothing more satisfying than cooking a whole fish.

I performed as a stand-up comedian. Once.

My previous job titles include copywriter, marketing director, and public address announcer.

I now have a job where I type CFCM after my name.

My fondness for the word "badassery" is somewhat alarming.

I learned how to write code at Code Fellows in Seattle.

My band submitted a video for NPR's Tiny Desk Concert contest. We didn't win.

Writing columns for ESPN.com was rad.

The world needs more Winston Wolfs and less Caillous.

Big ideas are currency.

I have been wearing these eyeglasses long before hipsters were a thing.
